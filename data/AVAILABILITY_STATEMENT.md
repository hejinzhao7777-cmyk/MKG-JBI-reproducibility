# Manuscript-ready availability statement

## Data Availability

The data analysed in this study are publicly available. TCGA molecular and clinical data were obtained from the NCI Genomic Data Commons (https://portal.gdc.cancer.gov/). Independent validation cohorts were obtained from the Gene Expression Omnibus under accession numbers GSE31210, GSE14520, GSE29609, GSE39582, GSE84437, GSE65858, GSE50081 and GSE76427, and from ArrayExpress under accession E-MTAB-1980. The processed analysis outputs and source data underlying the tables and figures are available in the accompanying repository at `https://github.com/[ACCOUNT]/MKG-JBI-reproducibility` and will be archived at [DOI TO BE ADDED AFTER DEPOSITION].
